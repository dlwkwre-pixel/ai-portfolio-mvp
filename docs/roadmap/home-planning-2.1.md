## Priority 2: Affordability Score
Score components (weighted):
- Housing Cost Ratio (25%)
- Down Payment Strength (20%)
- Price-to-Income Ratio (20%)
- Retirement Impact (20%)
- Break-even Fit (15%)

Ratings: 90-100 Excellent / 75-89 Comfortable / 60-74 Stretch / <60 High Risk

Status: Built in Sprint 2.1

## Future Vision

Home Planning should answer:
1. Should I buy?
2. Can I afford it?
3. What house can I afford?
4. How does this impact retirement?
5. How does this impact investments?
6. Is renting actually better?
7. What decision creates the most long-term wealth?

Focus on decision quality and financial planning integration before ZIP-code APIs, property APIs, or listing integrations.
